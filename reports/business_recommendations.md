# Business Recommendations — Churn Reduction Strategy

## Priority 1: Contract Conversion Program 🔴

**Finding:** Month-to-month customers churn at 42.7% vs 2.8% for two-year contract customers.

**Recommendation:**
- Offer 15–20% discount on first year of annual contract to month-to-month customers with tenure < 18 months
- Target via in-app notification, email sequence at months 3, 6, and 12
- Sales script: "Lock in your current rate for 12 months and save $X"

**Projected Impact:**
- If 20% of high-risk MTM customers convert → ~370 customers retained
- Revenue protected: ~$22,000/month, $264,000/year

---

## Priority 2: Early Tenure Retention Program 🔴

**Finding:** Average tenure at churn is 18.0 months. Churn risk peaks in months 6–18.

**Recommendation:**
- Automated "Customer Success" touchpoints at months 6, 12, and 18
- Proactive satisfaction survey at month 3 — flag low scores for immediate follow-up
- Month 12 loyalty reward (bill credit, free add-on month)

**Projected Impact:**
- Catching 30% of early-tenure churners before they cancel
- ~280 customers retained, ~$20,000/month revenue protected

---

## Priority 3: Fiber Optic Service Quality Improvement 🟡

**Finding:** Fiber optic customers churn at 41.9% — higher than DSL (19%) despite paying more.

**Recommendation:**
- Conduct service quality audit: uptime, speed consistency, support response times
- Implement SLA guarantees with bill credits for missed targets
- Create fiber-specific customer community for peer support

**Projected Impact:**
- Closing half the gap between fiber (41.9%) and DSL (19%) churn = ~580 customers retained
- Revenue protected: ~$50,000/month (fiber customers pay highest charges)

---

## Priority 4: Security & Support Bundling 🟡

**Finding:** Customers without OnlineSecurity or TechSupport churn at ~2x the rate of those with services.

**Recommendation:**
- Bundle OnlineSecurity + TechSupport free for first 3 months for all new customers
- Highlight security/support benefits in onboarding emails
- Create upsell touchpoint at month 2 with usage statistics

**Projected Impact:**
- Increases add-on adoption from ~30% to ~50%
- Secondary benefit: $5–10 additional monthly revenue per upsell

---

## Priority 5: Senior Citizen Retention Program 🟢

**Finding:** Senior citizens churn at 41.7% vs 26.5% overall.

**Recommendation:**
- Create "Senior Simplified" plan: fewer features, lower price, premium support
- Dedicated senior support phone line with longer service hours
- Partner with senior community organizations for education sessions

**Projected Impact:**
- Reduce senior churn from 41.7% to ~30%
- ~150 customers retained, ~$9,000/month revenue protected
