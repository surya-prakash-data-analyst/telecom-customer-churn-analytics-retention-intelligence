# Stakeholder Recommendations — Action Guide

## For: VP Customer Success

**Your KPI:** Reduce churn rate from 26.5% to under 22% within 12 months

**Top 3 Actions:**
1. Launch contract conversion program with 15% annual discount offer for MTM customers (months 3–18)
2. Fund automated early-tenure outreach at months 6, 12, 18
3. Commission fiber optic service quality audit

**Dashboard metrics to track monthly:**
- Churn rate by contract type
- Early-tenure (< 18mo) churn rate
- Fiber optic churn rate
- Contract conversion rate (MTM → Annual)

---

## For: Head of Revenue Operations

**Your KPI:** Protect $1M+ in annual recurring revenue

**Top 3 Actions:**
1. Prioritize retention budget toward high-revenue churners (fiber optic, $74+ monthly)
2. Build at-risk customer report using `sql/top_risk_customers.sql` risk scoring model
3. Track monthly revenue at risk (MTM customers, < 24 months tenure)

**Revenue KPIs to report:**
- Monthly revenue lost to churn
- Revenue at risk (high-risk segment, not yet churned)
- Revenue protected via retention interventions

---

## For: Head of Product / Service

**Your KPI:** Reduce fiber optic churn from 41.9% to under 30%

**Top 3 Actions:**
1. Service quality audit: uptime, speed, support response time for fiber customers
2. Implement SLA with bill credits for missed targets
3. Create fiber customer advisory panel to gather qualitative insights

---

## For: Marketing Team

**Your KPI:** Increase contract upgrade rate; support senior retention

**Top 3 Actions:**
1. Email/SMS campaign to MTM customers at months 3, 6, 12: contract upgrade offer
2. Senior-targeted simplified plan promotion
3. Add-on upsell campaign: OnlineSecurity + TechSupport bundling

---
